5. Account Deletion Process

Account Deletion for Onet

Since Onet uses Google Sign-In, account deletion is directly managed through your Google Account settings.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Google Account Management

    To remove access for Onet, go to your Google Account's Apps & Services section.

  2. Remove App Authorization

    Find Onet in the list of connected apps and click "Remove Access". This will disconnect the app from your Google Account.

  3. Uninstall the App

    Uninstall the Onet app from your device through Google Play Store or your device's app management settings.

Note: Removing app access does not automatically delete any game progress stored within the game itself. You may need to clear game data separately through your device's app settings.

6. Data Management

Data We Collect

For Onet, minimal data is collected:

  • Google Account ID for authentication
  • Basic profile information from Google Sign-In
  • Anonymous game performance and usage statistics

Data Deletion

When you remove app access:

  • Your Google Account connection is immediately revoked
  • No personal identifiable information is retained
  • Anonymized game statistics may be kept for performance analysis

Purchases and Data

Purchase records are managed entirely through Google Play Store. For purchase history or deletion, please contact Google Play Store support.
